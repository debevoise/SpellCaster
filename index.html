About

Whoa! What's that?

You almost missed it, a zonky spell just whizzed by your head. Spellcaster is an interactive text canvas where spells reign. A spell is a combination of one or more keywords that control it and other spells' movement, shape, color, and speed around the grid.

Click on Help if you're stuck and need some inspiration, or view all keywords at Cheatcodes.

Check out Spellcaster on Github. A project by Simon DeBevoise.

    Help

    What am I supposed to do?

    Type words to cast spells! To get started, typing Spell will generate a spell with four random keywords. Hit space or enter to initialize a new spell.

    Certain keywords will have different effects on a given spell. Up will make a word move up, blue will color it blue, and so forth. Keywords are chainable and can coexist with non-keywords, so "FOOSmallBAREmoji" will make the text smaller and use the emoji keyboard.

    Some keywords are special and can effect other spells:

    All will apply all previous keywords in the current spell to every spell on the grid. When everything goes haywire, Clear will give you a fresh start. Slow and Fast also operate globally.

    Use the arrow keys to move the cursor or to play Snake.

    For a comprehensive list of keywords, check out Index.
